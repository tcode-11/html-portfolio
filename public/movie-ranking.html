The Best Movies According to Takachancha

My Top 3 Movies I Have Watched This Year

The Lord of the Rings

This movie is so dynamic that it was really fun to watch!
Each story is deeply connected to each other, so you cannot even miss a single story.

Firebird

I watched Firebird in winter, and I felt I wanted to find someone that is meant to be bonded together.
Even though both characters wished to be together, the time and society tore them apart,
which made me so sad. I really recommend this movie for those who believe true love.

Love, Simon

In this movie, common things are depicted that young gay people struggle with in their childhood.
One high school boy in the movie is thought to be just an ordinary straight, but he actually isn't.
He starts messaging an unknown guy online and slowly falling love with him.
